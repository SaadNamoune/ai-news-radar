## Welcome to AI News Radar

**AI News Radar** is a fully automated daily digest of the most important news in AI, cybersecurity, and open-source — curated and summarized by **Mistral AI**.

Every day at 23:00 Algeria time, the pipeline:
1. Fetches articles from 20+ curated RSS feeds (HackerNews, OpenAI Blog, Krebs on Security, HuggingFace Papers...)
2. Scores and summarizes each article using Mistral AI
3. Generates and publishes this digest automatically
