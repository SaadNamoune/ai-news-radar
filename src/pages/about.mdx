## AI News Radar

**AI News Radar** is an automated pipeline that aggregates, scores, and summarizes the most important daily news in AI, cybersecurity, and open-source — powered by [Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai).

Every day at **23:00 Algeria time**, the system:
1. Fetches articles from 20+ curated RSS feeds
2. Scores each article using a language model (relevance, quality, novelty)
3. Generates concise English summaries
4. Publishes the digest automatically via GitHub Actions + Vercel
